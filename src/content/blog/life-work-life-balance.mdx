Remote work offers flexibility, but it also blurs the line between professional and personal life. Here's how to maintain balance.

## Set Boundaries

Define clear working hours and communicate them to your team. When the workday ends, close your laptop and step away.

## Create a Dedicated Workspace

Separate your work area from your living space. Even a small desk in a corner helps your brain switch between work and relaxation modes.

## Take Real Breaks

Step away from the screen during lunch. Go for a walk, read a book, or do something that doesn't involve pixels.

## Stay Connected

Remote work can be isolating. Schedule virtual coffee chats, join online communities, and maintain social connections.

## Listen to Your Body

Pay attention to signs of burnout. Fatigue, irritability, and decreased motivation are signals that something needs to change.
