A good morning routine sets the tone for the entire day. Here's what works for me.

## Wake Up Early

I wake up at 6:00 AM, giving myself two hours before work starts. This quiet time is invaluable for focused work and personal growth.

## No Phone Zone

The first 30 minutes of my day are phone-free. No emails, no social media, no notifications. This prevents reactive mode from taking over.

## Movement

A 15-minute stretch or light exercise routine wakes up the body and clears the mind. It doesn't need to be intense — consistency matters more than intensity.

## Planning

I spend 10 minutes reviewing my priorities for the day. What are the three most important tasks? What can wait until tomorrow?

## The Results

Since adopting this routine, my productivity has increased significantly. I feel more in control of my time and less reactive to external demands.
