After years of experimenting with productivity systems, here are the five techniques that consistently deliver results.

## 1. Time Blocking

Schedule specific blocks of time for specific tasks. This prevents context switching and deepens focus.

## 2. The Pomodoro Technique

Work in focused 25-minute intervals with 5-minute breaks. After four pomodoros, take a longer break.

## 3. Eat the Frog

Tackle your most difficult task first thing in the morning. Everything else will feel easier by comparison.

## 4. Weekly Review

Spend 30 minutes every Friday reviewing what worked, what didn't, and planning the next week.

## 5. Digital Minimalism

Unsubscribe from unnecessary emails, turn off non-essential notifications, and batch-process messages twice a day.

The secret isn't finding the perfect system — it's consistently applying a good one.
